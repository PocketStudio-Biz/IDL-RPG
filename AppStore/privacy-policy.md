## Introduction

IDL RPG ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application IDL RPG (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### No Personal Information Collected
IDL RPG does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app is designed to work entirely offline and does not require user accounts or authentication.

### Local Data Storage
All game progress, settings, and data are stored locally on your device. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

### No Third-Party Services
IDL RPG does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use, share, or sell any user data.

## Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS/macOS data storage mechanisms. We do not transmit any data over the internet.

## Children's Privacy

IDL RPG is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App Store listing. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete. All game data remains on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
